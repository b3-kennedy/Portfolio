Turret Defence

About

Turret Defence is a 2 player online multiplayer roguelike game made with Unity where players play as turrets and have to defend a wall from waves of enemies. At the end of each wave the players are offered upgrades, the catch being that as well as giving the player a positive effect the upgrades will also give a negative effect to the other player, meaning that players will have to communicate effectively to become as strong as possible.
